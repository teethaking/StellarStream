These two screens showcase the Institutional Rebalancing workflow within the Sovereign Quantitative ecosystem. This is where high-level strategy—like moving $2,450,000.00—meets strict multi-signature (multi-sig) governance.

1. The Strategy: Split Draft #821
This view details exactly how a large pool of capital is being redirected from Cold Storage Vault 04 (on Ethereum Mainnet) into active yield-bearing positions.

Asset Allocation Logic:

45.00% ($1,102,500): Yield Generation via Aave v3 auto-compounding.

35.00% ($857,500): Liquidity Provision via Uniswap v3 (USDC/ETH pair).

20.00% ($490,000): Operational Treasury for a "Daily Expense Buffer."

Execution Details: Shows a gas limit optimization of 250,000 units, highlighting the technical precision required for these large on-chain transactions.

2. The Governance: Approval Workflow
These screens illustrate the "human-in-the-loop" security protocol. To move nearly $2.5M, the system requires a 3/4 Quorum (at least 3 out of 4 authorized council members).

Signature Status:

Confirmed: Director of Risk and Compliance Officer have already signed.

Pending: The Treasury Lead is currently required to sign to hit the 3/4 threshold.

Awaiting: The Chief Investment Officer/Legal Counsel are next in line if the quorum isn't met.

The "Nudge" Feature: A specialized UI element allows administrators to "Nudge User" (Risk Admin) to keep the proposal moving through the pipeline.

Gated Access: One screen shows the "Waiting for Council" state. For users without signing authority, the screen is locked, offering only "Read-Only Access" to view terms or export the draft.

Design Continuity
Consistency: The use of the Inter font and a dark, high-contrast palette maintains the institutional "Cyber-Fintech" feel seen in the previous monitoring tools.

Auditability: The "Audit JSON" button ensures that while the UI is clean and visual, the underlying smart contract data is always accessible for technical verification.
