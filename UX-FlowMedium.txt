This image illustrates the Transaction Confirmation interface for the Digital Ledger platform. The UI is designed to provide high-clarity verification of "transaction intent" before a user commits to a digital asset transfer.
1. Transaction Intent & Verification
The central modal focuses on transparency and security to prevent accidental or malicious transfers.

Secure Node Badge: Indicates that the transaction is being routed through a verified, secure node.

Flow Visualization: A clear directional arrow shows the movement from "Your Wallet" to the recipient "@USERNAME".

Asset Breakdown: Explicitly highlights the asset being sent: 50.00 USDC.

Smart Contract Audit: A "Verified Smart Contract" banner confirms that the underlying Nebula V2 contract passed a security audit on September 24, 2023.2.
