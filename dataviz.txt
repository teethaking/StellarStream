The Global Flow interface provides a "God-eye view" of institutional movement across a distributed network of bridge nodes. It tracks massive volume—over $1.4 billion in the last 24 hours—with extremely high throughput.

Pulsing nodes and dotted lines visualize the origin and destination of liquidity across continents.
